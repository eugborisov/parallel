Our Mission

From self-driving cars to delivery drones, autonomous systems have the opportunity to drastically improve the quality of life for billions of people. We enable our customers to develop their technology in safe virtual environments, allowing them to deploy in a fraction of the time and for a fraction of the cost.

The Parallel Domain Story

We are a fast-growing startup based in Palo Alto, California. Founded in 2017, we have quickly assembled a world-class team that is already changing the way the industry simulates. Our software is a world generation engine that makes creating new simulation environments as easy as turning a dial. We are here so that you can focus on preparing your technology for the rigors of the real world.

Meet the Leadership

Kevin McNamara

Founder & CEO

Kevin brings deep computer graphics experience having built and led a team within Apple's Special Projects Group focused on autonomous systems simulation. Previously, he architected and implemented procedural content systems for Microsoft Game Studios and contributed to academy award winning films at Pixar Animation Studios.

James Grieve

CTO

With over 20 years of experience in the video game and tech industries at companies such as Apple, Electronic Arts and United Front Games, James brings an extensive knowledge of real-time graphics and engineering management to the world of autonomous vehicle simulation. He has led teams building world-class, AAA games such as Need for Speed, Sleeping Dogs and ModNation: Racers along with many others.

Our Team

We hail from world's top autonomous systems, graphics, simulation, and management teams from Apple, Pixar, Microsoft, Electronic Arts, Lucasfilm, Sony, McKinsey, and more. Our extensive experience in content generation and virtual world building enable us to develop the industry leading content generation solution.

More importantly, we obsess over the application of simulation to the betterment of revolutionary technology.

Head of Sales

We are looking for a Head of Sales to help Parallel Domain continue growing the customer base and refining the sales process. As Head of Sales, you will report directly to the CEO. You will be responsible for managing and drivings sales, from developing leads to closing deals. You will be the voice of the customer, distilling feedback from potential customers and sharing that with our product and engineering teams. In addition, you will be tasked with refining the current sales processes and playbook to lay the foundation for Parallel Domain’s continued growth.

Requirements
  • We are looking for a highly motivated ‘hunter’, who excels in a collaborative, dynamic environment
  • 3-5+ years inside sales experience, ideally in the enterprise software and/or automotive sectors, with consistent track record of exceeding quota
Bonus points
  • Ability and willingness to learn a technical product and new industry
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Customer-empathy mindset to act as a customer advocate for product development
  • Experience developing and/or refining a scalable sales process to scale a sales organization
A day in the life...
  • Managing the full sales cycle
  • Building industry relationships
  • Being our customers’ advocate
  • Helping our customers win
  • Collaborating with teammates on the strategic direction of PD’s offerings
  • And much, much more!
